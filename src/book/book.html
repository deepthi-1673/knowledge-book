field guide

  • Open / fold a note — click its title.
  • Move a note to another notebook — drag its dotted grip onto a notebook tab in the sidebar, or use move to → inside the note.
  • Merge two notes — drag one note's grip onto another note, drop, confirm.
  • Save together — when you save again from a page you've saved from before, the on-page toast offers add to "…" so highlights land in one note.
  • Read text from a screenshot — snip anything (a PDF, a slide) and press Ctrl+V here; it becomes a text note (needs your API key).
  • Ask — the bar at the top answers questions from your own notes.
