field guide

  • Open / fold a note — click its title.
  • Move a note to another notebook — drag its dotted grip onto a notebook tab in the sidebar, or use move to → inside the note.
  • Merge two notes — drag one note's grip onto another note, drop, confirm.
  • Save together — when you save again from a page you've saved from before, the on-page toast offers add to "…" so highlights land in one note.
  • Read text from a screenshot — snip anything (a PDF, a slide) and press Ctrl+V here; it becomes a text note (needs your API key).
  • Ask — the bar at the top answers questions from your own notes.

Your book is empty

On any page, highlight something worth keeping and click the Save to journal button (or right-click → Save selection to Knowledge Book). Your notes will collect here.

welcome to your knowledge book

Highlight text on any page — an AI chat, docs, an article — and click the green Save to journal button that pops up. Or right-click a selection (or an image) and pick Save to Knowledge Book.

reading your notes

Notes fold to a title + short preview — click a title to open or fold it. Tables, lists, code, and images are kept exactly as captured.

notebooks, moving & merging

Make notebooks with + new. Drag a note's dotted grip onto a notebook tab to move it — or onto another note to merge them. Save twice from the same page and the toast offers add to "…" so one page stays one note.

optional AI powers

Add your own OpenRouter key in Settings to unlock Ask (answers from your own notes), summaries, explain / example / quiz on any note, and screenshot → text: snip anything and press Ctrl+V right here.

never locked in

Everything is Markdown. Send notes to Obsidian, export the whole book to any folder, or download a JSON backup in Settings. Happy collecting!
